Sonnet

My dearest, Juli

I always thought that love had to have a plethora of reasons, but I realized that I only needed one.

It has been 1 year, 12 months, 52 weeks, 365 days, 8,760 hours, 525,600 minutes, or estimately 3.154 x 107 seconds that we both have seated ourselves in this one roller coaster of an adventure.

“You changed everything the day you walked into my life” is a quote by A.L. Jackson which the Nino 2 years ago wouldn’t think of ever using. From the point in time you fully entered my life, it felt like time went slower and faster at the same time—the time we were apart felt like an eternity, yet the year we successfully spent together was a halcyon that was faster than a heartbeat.

Before you stepped in, my life was already set in stone. I was a closed system, running at my own pace. But you didn’t just waltz in; you completely collided with my entire program. You were the single variable that I have never accounted for.

In a world that asks me to become a programmer, student, athlete, and a model, you are the only person I can be me. I still remember the nights where the pressure of being all those things felt suffocating. But then you came along.

You were the first person who didn't need me to be perfect. Whenever I'm with you, I'm not afraid of being seen. Now the only thing I’m afraid of is going back to a world where your voice isn’t here to bring me peace.

I know that I am not the best partner, student, athlete, listener, talker, or thinker... but I'll be the best imperfect partner for you. I promise that every update, every sprint, and every change of my life will be done with you in my mind. I'm working towards a life where Elijah, Emery, Elizabeth, and Emilia are alive with you as our source of love.

Just like everyone has their reasons for love, I realized that I only needed one, and that sole reason is reading this letter right now.

Yours and forever yours,
Nino
